# Overview

A hero section is the heart and soul of the company. It is the first change of engagement between you and the potential user on the other side of the screen. Users will instantly get a feel for your company or product based on the colours you've chosen, the fonts you used and by how playful or sophisticated or you landing page is.

Utilising this amazing opportunity to convince visitors that your product is what they need to solve their problems, you must capture them immediately. First impressions are made within the first 15 seconds.

Use a title to tell your important message, include buttons for them to get started straight away or learn more, incorporate your companies colours and brands into this section and let it do its magic.
