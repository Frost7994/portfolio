# Overview

Feature sections on landing pages give the company a real opportunity to sell their key features to visitors, giving them a reason to use your product. Keeping these sections to a minimum while also displaying the core of the product can sometimes be difficult.

Below I have created some sample designs of feature sections that allow you to quickly get an idea what the product is or what problem the product is trying to solve. Including a testimony or CTA button in these sections can also help conversion rates.
